## REGULATORY REQUIREMENT

The HIPAA Security Rule (45 CFR §164.308(a)(2)) is a **required implementation specification** that mandates:

## RESPONSIBILITIES

The Security Officer shall be responsible for:

### Strategic Responsibilities

1. **Security Program Strategy**
   - Develop and maintain a comprehensive security program
   - Align security initiatives with business objectives
   - Establish security metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs)
   - Report security program status to leadership quarterly

2. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - Ensure compliance with HIPAA Security Rule requirements
   - Stay current on security regulations and industry standards
   - Coordinate with Privacy Officer on overlapping requirements
   - Maintain documentation for regulatory audits

3. **Risk Assessment & Management**
   - Conduct annual security risk assessments (required)
   - Identify and prioritize security risks
   - Develop risk mitigation and remediation plans
   - Track risk remediation progress

### Operational Responsibilities

4. **Policy & Procedure Management**
   - Create and maintain security policies and procedures
   - Review policies annually (minimum)
   - Communicate policy updates to workforce
   - Ensure policies reflect current practices and regulations

5. **Security Controls Implementation**
   - Implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
   - Monitor effectiveness of security controls
   - Recommend control enhancements
   - Document control implementation

6. **Incident Response & Breach Management**
   - Serve as Incident Commander for security incidents
   - Lead breach investigations and risk assessments
   - Coordinate breach notifications as required
   - Document incidents and lessons learned

7. **Security Awareness & Training**
   - Ensure all workforce members receive security training
   - Provide specialized training for IT staff and high-risk roles
   - Conduct periodic security awareness campaigns
   - Track training completion rates

8. **Security Monitoring & Auditing**
   - Monitor security logs and alerts
   - Conduct periodic security audits
   - Investigate security violations
   - Recommend disciplinary actions for policy violations

### Collaborative Responsibilities

9. **Cross-Functional Coordination**
   - Work with Privacy Officer on PHI protection
   - Coordinate with IT on technical implementations
   - Partner with HR on workforce security
   - Collaborate with Legal on compliance matters
   - Engage with Risk Management on enterprise risk

10. **Stakeholder Communication**
    - Report security metrics to executive leadership
    - Communicate security requirements to workforce
    - Interface with auditors and assessors
    - Respond to security inquiries from business associates
